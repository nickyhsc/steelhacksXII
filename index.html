Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to support women in STEM, with a focus on women in computer science, and to raise awareness about the issues that affect them. We also want to provide women with resources to help them succeed in STEM. We will do this by analyzing statistics that affect women in STEM and providing women with resources to help them learn to code.
